Creative Services

Ghostwriting

Screenwriting

Screenwriting services are available for short films, feature films, television concepts, pilots, web series, and other visual storytelling projects.

Collaboration

Some projects are stronger when they are built through active collaboration.

GhostWrite Studios can work alongside creators to brainstorm, develop ideas, solve story problems, build fictional worlds, and shape projects from early concepts into complete creative works.

AI Decontamination

Artificial intelligence can generate material quickly, but the results often feel repetitive, generic, inconsistent, or disconnected from the creator's actual voice.

AI decontamination focuses on rewriting and refining AI-assisted material so that it reads naturally, maintains a consistent voice, avoids obvious machine-generated patterns, and better reflects the creator's original intent.

Editing and Rewriting

Existing work can be reviewed for clarity, flow, grammar, pacing, tone, consistency, and overall effectiveness.

Editing services may range from careful line-level revisions to substantial rewrites designed to strengthen the entire project.

Story Structure

Strong ideas can lose their impact when the structure is not supporting them.

Story-structuring services help organize plot points, character arcs, conflict, pacing, themes, reveals, and emotional turning points into a coherent narrative.

Character Development

Memorable characters need more than names and descriptions. They need motivations, flaws, relationships, contradictions, and meaningful choices.

Character-development services can help create new characters or deepen existing ones through stronger personalities, histories, dialogue, conflicts, and arcs.
