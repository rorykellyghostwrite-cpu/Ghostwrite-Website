Screenwriting

Screenwriting remains one of the foundations of GhostWrite Studios. My work focuses on character-driven stories, strong thematic development, believable dialogue, and carefully structured narratives designed for film and television.

Multiple screenplays have been submitted to the Screamfest Horror Film Festival, one of the industry's premier showcases for independent horror filmmaking. Every screenplay has been another opportunity to refine craft, strengthen storytelling, and continue developing as a writer.
