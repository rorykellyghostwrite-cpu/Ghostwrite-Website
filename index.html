Welcome to GhostWrite Studios

GhostWrite Studios develops original tabletop games, screenplays, fiction, and professional writing services.

Building Worlds, One Story at a Time.

GhostWrite Studios is an independent intellectual property studio founded by Rory Kelly in 2025. We develop original tabletop games, feature screenplays, and narrative worlds designed to become lasting creative franchises.

What We Create

GhostWrite Studios exists to create original stories across multiple mediums. From tabletop gaming to film, every project begins with one goal: build memorable worlds and unforgettable characters that audiences want to revisit.

🎲 Genome: Apex Shifting

An original strategic tabletop card game currently in development. Genome blends tactical decision making with evolving creatures and competitive play, serving as the studio's first original game IP.

🎬 Screenwriting

GhostWrite Studios is developing multiple original feature films, including entries planned for Screamfest 2026. The focus is on elevated genre storytelling that combines strong characters with memorable concepts.

✍️ Creative Development

Beyond original productions, GhostWrite Studios provides ghostwriting, editing, story development, and creative consulting for authors, filmmakers, and independent creators.

Meet the Founder

Rory Kelly is the Founder and Creative Director of GhostWrite Studios. After years working as a professional chef, he left the kitchen to pursue storytelling full time, focusing on screenwriting, tabletop game design, and the creation of original intellectual property.

Today, GhostWrite Studios serves as the home for Rory's growing portfolio of creative work, bringing together film, games, writing, and future multimedia projects under one independent studio.
